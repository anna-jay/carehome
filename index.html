Supportive and dignified care

Honeymeadow Care Home

Honeymeadow Care is fully committed to providing care homes which offer high quality, supportive and dignified care to our clients whilst also being a good place for staff to work. We have 39 care homes, each one staffed by diligent, caring and trustworthy personnel who keep our clients and their families happiness as a core priority.

Our Services

We offer short term Respite Care in addition to long term care. Care is individualised and based on a person's life history, whilst also involving and empowering their families. We ensure that a resident's current strengths and abilities form the basis of their care, rather than focusing solely on their weaknesses or disabilities.

Home Life

With their attractive settings, modern facilities and varied, imaginative agenda of activities - our care homes offer a quality of life that's second to none. We make sure our residents are able to maintain and exceed the lifestyles they've become accustomed to.
